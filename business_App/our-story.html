Our Story

The philosophy behind Aurum & Time

Born from a Simple Belief

We believe that exceptional design and quality materials shouldn't be locked behind traditional luxury markups. Aurum & Time was founded to challenge the notion that you need to spend a fortune to own beautiful, lasting accessories.

By partnering directly with artisans and cutting out middlemen, we deliver premium watches and demi-fine jewelry at honest prices.

Sustainability at Our Core

Every piece is crafted using recycled precious metals and ethically sourced stones. Our packaging is plastic-free, and we're committed to carbon-neutral shipping by 2027.

We visit our partner workshops regularly to ensure fair wages, safe conditions, and exceptional craftsmanship.
